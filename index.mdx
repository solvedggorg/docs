# Hello

Welcome to **solved.gg** docs — the documentation home for language tooling and related products built by [iResolved, LLC](https://solved.gg).

This site covers product documentation and the legal terms that apply to our software and services. Use the **product switcher** in the navbar to open a product’s docs. Product guides live under their own paths (for example, [rusty](/rusty)).
